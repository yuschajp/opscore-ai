How it works

From query to cited answer in under a second.

01 — Embed
Query vectorized
Your question is embedded using OpenAI's text-embedding-3-small model and compared against every chunk in the vault.
02 — Retrieve
Top chunks surfaced
ChromaDB returns the 5 most semantically relevant SOP passages, filtered by doc type and scored by cosine similarity.
03 — Generate
Claude reasons over context
Claude Sonnet receives the retrieved passages and your query. It cites the source document in every answer — no hallucination of rules or procedures.
04 — Stream
Response delivered live
Tokens stream to the UI via SSE as they generate. Source pills appear at the end, linking back to the exact SOP section.
